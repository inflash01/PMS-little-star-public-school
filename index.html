Building Brighter Futures

Little Star Public School is a prestigious educational institution committed to excellence in education. We provide a nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and discover their potential.

Located in the heart of Buxar, Bihar, we offer comprehensive education from Play Group to Class 5 following the CBSE pattern with modern teaching methodologies.
